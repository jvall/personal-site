Work

Ever since starting to develop with the LAMP stack back in middle school, John continues to be amazed at how much the web continues to progress. His expertise involves creating responsive, progressive, and beautiful web apps with technologies like Angular, React, Node, and .NET.

Sample development projects include:

  • Sophisticated restaurant management application suite for food services software vendor (AWS, React, modern CSS/Sass)
  • Hybrid web/iOS training application for large plywood and veneer manufacturer (Angular, Swift, Office 365)
  • Analytics and reporting dashboard for healthcare services provider (AngularJS, .NET)

About

A native midwesterner, John grew up in Carmel, IN, and graduated from Purdue University with a honors degree in Computer Science and the Spanish language. After spending two summers interning and two others in Spain, he now works for West Monroe Partners to help clients address their needs by building robust and secure custom applications.

He serves as a football and basketball referee for the IHSA and works across the Chicagoland area.

In his free time, he enjoys swing dancing, biking, trying new restaurants, and thinking of where he'll travel next. He's involved in his church and volunteers his time in an after-school program for at-risk youth.

John does not normally talk in the third person.
